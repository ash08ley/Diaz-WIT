What is the feature of Casio watch?

Many Casio watches have multiple functions, such as a stopwatch, alarm, and countdown timer,

making them versatile and useful for various activities.

They also have features such as world time, dual time, and multiple alarms,

which makes them perfect for travelers and people who are always on the go.
